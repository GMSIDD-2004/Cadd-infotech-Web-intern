PLACEMENTS


CADD INFOTECH offers 100% placement assistance to Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering & Architecture fresh students who take admission for our Advance Diploma & CADDINFOTECH Career Programs. We have a dedicated Training & Placement Team supported by team of core Technical Support Faculty. Once student takes the admission, his / her credentials are registered in our Training & Placement portal and all the job updates are sent through the same portal. We train our students on basics of Engineering Design, Drawing Reading, various Design & Analysis Software's used in Mechanical / Civil / Architecture & Electrical industries along with Soft Skills training. Mock interviews and tests are conducted prior to final interviews of Students. We invite industrial experienced resources and our Alumni to train our existing students so that they can clear the recruitment process. In few cases, we train our students as per company job requirements at no extra cost. Interviews are scheduled through Campus placement and at company locations. We also offer placement assistance to experienced candidates who wish to upgrade their jobs through the courses completed by them at CADD INFOTECH. For Modular Courses, we provide only placement references to the students.
