ABOUT US

About The Training Institute

CADD INFOTECH is a global leader in delivering technical training. Our training portfolio includes CAD/CAM/CAE, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical and Architecture Design & Analysis courses. We offer Classroom and Instructor-Led Online trainings on all the Engineering Design & Analysis Software's used in the industries. CADD INFOTECH provides 100% Placement Assistance to fresh Mechanical, Civil, Electrical Engineering & Architecture students who enroll for CADD INFOTECH Certified Career Programs training at our centers.We have a dedicated Training & Placement Cell for our Students which caters Engineering Fundamentals & Soft Skills trainings to our students along with their campus placements. Our Classroom and Instructor-Led Online courses are developed and delivered by Working Professionals from industry and academic. Our primary focus is on Skill Development Programs for Mechanical, Civil, Electrical Engineering & Architecture students by arranging finishing school job oriented training programs for them. We are working on Vocational Training Programs of engineering education through specific training programs and the evolution of credit framework. We offer various customized training programs to companies as per their requirements. We have successfully delivered Corporate Training Programs on various Software Tools. Teaching & Learning process followed is at par with the quality norms and we take dedicated efforts for the placement of our students and guide them for their future career.


VISION

As the world becomes increasingly interconnected, the need for globally-minded professionals have never been greater. At the heart of this need is the desire to create positive change on a global scale. To achieve this, it is essential to develop world-class professionals who have the skills, knowledge, and expertise to make a positive impact in the global community. This mission requires a comprehensive approach to education that goes beyond simply imparting knowledge. It involves nurturing critical thinking skills, fostering creativity, and cultivating a deep understanding of the complex challenges facing our world today. It also requires a commitment to diversity and inclusion, as well as a dedication to ethical leadership and social responsibility. By developing world-class professionals who are completely equipped to make a positive impact in the global community, we can create a better future for all. These professionals will be at the forefront of solving some of the most pressing issues facing our world today, from climate change to poverty alleviation to global health crises. They will be the change-makers, innovators, and leaders who will shape the world for generations to come. In short, developing world-class professionals with a global mindset is not just a mission, it is a necessity. It is essential if we are to create a better world for ourselves, our children, and the generations that follow.
